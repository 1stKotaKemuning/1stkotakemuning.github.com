About

The Boys' Brigade is a uniform society formed in 1883. It is one of the first and oldest uniform youth organization which is still up and running all around the world

Our company 1st Kota Kemuning cosisted of about 30 teenagers when it was first founded in 30th June 2012 in the beautiful country of Malaysia. However, after all these years, we are now rocking up to 50 brigadiers and are growing strong.

What are we strive to Build

Discipline

One of the core fundemental of The Boys' Brigade is the growth of ones discipline, confidence and self-respect.

Community

It is global and had influence many lives and people, thus forming a long-lasting community like none other.

Christianity

We aim the develop the true christian character among all members alike as we serve God.

Friendship

Through the course of many brigadiers journey, there will be definitely an endless bond between friends and the organisation itself.
